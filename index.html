Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11th March, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Science Box App collects, uses, and protects information when students and users use our mobile application. The application is designed to help Senior High School (SHS) students practice science practical experiments and quizzes for educational purposes.

Information We Collect

Our app may collect certain information to improve the learning experience. This includes:

  • Basic account information such as username and email during login.
  • Usage information such as quiz scores, progress, and interactions with the app.

How We Use the Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide access to the application's learning features.
  • Track student quiz performance and progress.
  • Improve the quality and performance of the app.
  • Fix technical issues and enhance user experience.

Internet Usage

The application requires an internet connection to access learning materials, quizzes, and updates. Internet connectivity is necessary for proper functionality of the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal information to others. User data is only used to operate and improve the educational services provided by the app.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Children’s Privacy

This application is designed for educational purposes and may be used by students. We encourage students to use the application under the guidance of teachers or parents where appropriate.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
